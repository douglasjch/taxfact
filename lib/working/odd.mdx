Vectigal urinae, or the urine tax, was a tax imposed in ancient Rome on the collection and sale of urine [urine tax](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pecunia_non_olet)

The Townshend Acts were a series of British laws passed in 1767 aimed at taxing the American colonies [Townshend Acts](https://www.britannica.com/event/Townshend-Acts)

Catasto is the Italian system of land registration. Catasto is the basis for the IMU council property tax (Imposta Municipale Unica). [Catasto](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Catasto)

The gabelle French salt tax [salt tax](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gabelle)

The alcabala or alcavala was a Spanish sales tax of up to fourteen percent, at each transaction[Alcabala](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Alcabala)

The Saladin Tithe was a tax levied in 1188 to fund the Third Crusade [Saladin Tithe](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Saladin_Tithe)

Danegeld was a tax raised to pay tribute or protection money to the Viking raiders to save a land from being ravaged[Danegeld](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Danegeld)
